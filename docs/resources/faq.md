# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions.

## General Questions

### How do I search the knowledge base?
Use the search bar at the top of any page. You can search by keywords, phrases, or topics.

### How often is the knowledge base updated?
Content is updated regularly. Check the "Last updated" date on individual pages for specific information.

### How can I suggest improvements?
Contact the knowledge base maintainers with your suggestions, or submit feedback through the established channels.

## Content Questions

### What if I find outdated information?
Please report it to the knowledge base maintainers so it can be updated.

### Can I contribute to the knowledge base?
Yes! Contributions are welcome. See the contribution guidelines for how to submit content.

### What if I can't find what I'm looking for?
1. Try different search terms
2. Browse the category structure
3. Check the glossary for terminology help
4. Contact the maintainers for assistance

## Technical Questions

### How do I report a broken link?
Submit an issue through the feedback system, noting the page URL and the broken link.

### Can I download content for offline use?
Contact the knowledge base maintainers about offline access options.
